## Business Recommendations

### Insights for Laptop as Preferred Device
- Users with their preferred location type as **Trekking** have ~30% higher proportion of taking the company’s product compared to those who don’t prefer trekking.  
- **Adult users (Adult_flag = 1)** have ~10% higher chance of taking the company’s product.  
- Among location types (Other, Movie, Financial), users preferring **Financial** type have the highest proportion (~30% higher) of taking the company’s product.  

### Insights for Mobile as Preferred Device
- Users with their preferred location type as **Trekking** have ~20% higher proportion of taking the company’s product.  
- **Adult users (Adult_flag = 1)** have ~20% higher chance of taking the company’s product.  
- Users who prefer **Hill Stations** have ~30% higher proportion of taking the company’s product compared to other location types.  
- For the location type **OTT**, the proportion of users taking the company’s product is lower compared to other location types.  

## Business Insights and Conclusion
- It is important to target customers wrongly predicted as converted to improve sales, but this should be secondary priority depending on budget.  
- Laptop users with mediocre/low social network scores, high travel networking scores, employed status, and preference for **Hill Stations** show much higher conversion rates. These profiles should be prioritized in targeted campaigns.  
- Mobile users who are employed adults with low popularity ratings, living in metro areas or PG/entertainment places, and enjoying entertainment activities (trekking, movies, OTT content) are most likely to buy travel tickets. Focused digital marketing campaigns should be directed at these profiles to maximize conversions.
